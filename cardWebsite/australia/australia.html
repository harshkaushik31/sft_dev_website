About Australia

Australia, the smallest continent and one of the largest countries on Earth, lies between the Pacific and Indian oceans in the Southern Hemisphere. Its capital city is Canberra, and its largest city is Sydney. Australia is known for its diverse landscapes, unique wildlife, and vibrant cities.

Australia is famous for its natural wonders, including the Great Barrier Reef, the Outback, and beautiful beaches. The country has a rich indigenous culture and a history of British colonization. Modern Australia is a multicultural society with a strong economy, known for its high quality of life, outdoor lifestyle, and friendly people.
