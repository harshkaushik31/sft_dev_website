About Russia

Russia, the largest country in the world, spans Eastern Europe and northern Asia. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and historical significance. The capital city is Moscow, and the official language is Russian.

Russia is famous for its iconic landmarks such as the Kremlin, Red Square, and St. Basil's Cathedral. The country has a diverse climate, ranging from arctic conditions in Siberia to temperate climates in the European part. Russia has a rich history, marked by the rise and fall of empires, revolutions, and significant contributions to art, literature, and science.
