About UAE

The United Arab Emirates (UAE) is a country in Western Asia located at the eastern end of the Arabian Peninsula. It borders Oman and Saudi Arabia and has maritime borders in the Persian Gulf with Qatar and Iran. The UAE is a federation of seven emirates, with Abu Dhabi as the capital and Dubai as the largest city. The country is known for its modern architecture, luxury shopping, and vibrant nightlife.

The UAE is a major hub for tourism, finance, and trade, attracting millions of visitors each year. It offers a unique blend of traditional culture and modernity, with attractions ranging from ancient forts and mosques to futuristic skyscrapers and world-class entertainment venues.
